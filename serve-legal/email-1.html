What are AllergyWise Audits?

AllergyWise® Audits offer a comprehensive service that provides businesses with robust and independent compliance testing. Through this programme, food establishments across the UK can assess and enhance their allergen management to provide safer dining experiences for all customers.

How We Help

Each audit offers businesses valuable insights into on-site customer experiences. Our tailored process evaluates whether staff proactively address allergens, provide accurate information, and ensure customers feel welcomed and well-cared for.
